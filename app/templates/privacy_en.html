Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Overview

OxePass is a password generator that fully respects your privacy. Our policy is simple: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Generated passwords exist only during request processing and are discarded immediately.

2. Data we do not collect

OxePass does not collect, log, or store:

  • — Generated passwords
  • — Email addresses or any personally identifiable information
  • — Usage history or preferences
  • — Tracking cookies or session identifiers
  • — Location data

3. Server logs

Like any web server, the OxePass server may automatically log basic access information such as IP address, browser used, and request date and time. These logs are used exclusively for operational and security purposes, never to track or identify users, and are periodically discarded.

4. Third-party services

OxePass uses the following external services, which may log information when loading resources:

  • Google Fonts

    We load the Inter and JetBrains Mono fonts directly from Google Fonts. When accessing the site, your browser makes a request to Google's servers to download the fonts. See Google's privacy policy.

  • Alpine.js and HTMX (CDN)

    We load the Alpine.js (via jsDelivr) and HTMX (via unpkg) libraries from external CDNs, which may generate requests to those servers.

We have no control over the data these third parties may collect. We recommend reading each one's privacy policy.

5. Cookies

OxePass does not use cookies of its own. There is no session tracking, personalized advertising, or any identification mechanism between visits.

6. Password generation security

Passwords are generated on the server using cryptographically secure randomization algorithms. All processing occurs in memory and values are discarded immediately after being sent to the browser. No password is persisted on any storage medium.

7. Minors

OxePass is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from minors. Since we do not collect any personal data, the service poses no risk in this regard.

8. Changes to this policy

We reserve the right to update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
